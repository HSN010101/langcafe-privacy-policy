Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to LangCafe! Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By accessing or using our App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you.

a. Personal Information

When you create an account or use certain features of the App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name: First and last name.
  • Date of Birth: To verify age and provide age-appropriate content.
  • Email Address: For account creation, communication, and verification.
  • Phone Number: For account security and communication purposes.
  • Gender: Optional information to tailor user experience.
  • Country Code: To determine your location and provide localized content.
  • Languages:
    • Known Languages: Languages you are proficient in.
    • Learning Languages: Languages you are interested in learning.
  • Self-Introduction: A brief bio you provide to other users.
  • Profile and Cover Photos: Images you upload to personalize your profile.

b. Content Information

- Posts: Text and images you create and share within the App.
- Images: Photos you upload as part of your posts or profile.
- Audio Data: Real-time audio communication facilitated by Agora SDK for language learning sessions.

c. Usage Data

- Log Information: Details of how you use the App, such as features accessed, time and duration of use, and actions performed.
- Device Information: Information about the device you use to access the App, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, verify your email, and ensure the security of your account.
  • Personalization: To tailor content and recommendations based on your preferences, languages, and activity.
  • Communication: To send you updates, notifications, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Content Sharing: To facilitate sharing of posts, images, and participation in audio sessions.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's features and functionality.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Third-Party Service Providers:
    • Firebase & Firestore: For authentication, data storage, and backend services.
    • Agora SDK: For facilitating real-time audio communication.
    • Google Cloud Services: For infrastructure and other backend services.
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

Note: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of receiving promotional communications.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

LangCafe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
